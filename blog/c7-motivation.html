"Delivering happiness"

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

The Pleasure, Passion Purpose lens is how we experience each of these and what brings us most happiness. I think how they described it was accurate to how most people live their lives. The first P is Pleasure Most people seek out and want pleasure the first, which is often short lived and unsustainable. It has been said that in order to be successful and have fulfilled happpiness you must do the things in life that are most sustainable, so unfortunately as pleasure does not last long, it is the least sustainable. The next P is Passion, this is described as flow, where this is both effective and enjoyable and therefore things just flow.. and time flies by. The most sustainable of all is Purpose. In order to feel completely satisfied and happy in life you need to have a higher purpose, something that is much bigger than you.
